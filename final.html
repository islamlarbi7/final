About Me

Here’s a bit about me and my journey as a developer.

After obtaining my high school diploma, I became interested in computer science, but the studies weren’t exactly what I was hoping for. So, my journey as a web developer began when I saw a post on Instagram about a school offering web development exercises with a private coach. I decided to enroll, and I spoke with the school supervisor to discuss all the details, including registration fees, the benefits of completing the exercises, and the certificate. After enrolling, I started attending weekly sessions, where we had weekly challenges to complete. This continued for three months, and finally, the time came to complete my final project and present it to the coach for evaluation to receive my certificate.

"And finally, if you want to get exercises from this school or know if the trainers are good there, you can contact me."
